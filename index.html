About Me

Welcome to the portfolio of software & website developer, Adam Steven.

I am a highly focused 4th year computing student, soon to be graduating from Abertay University with a predicted 1st class honours degree. Seeking to improve the groundwork I have built up during my time at University. As well as, developing experience in a professional work environment.

Research oriented, I value both maturing my independent and team-based skills through tackling and overcoming tasks set before me. Learning from those around me and adapting to new circumstances that arise.

The about me section below highlights aspects of my past and present to provide a better idea of me as a person.

Charity Work

Oxfam Volunteer

I was a Volunteer at Oxfam’s clothes retail store, Troon. Preparing donated clothes and items at the back of the shop, along with shelf stocking and helping with some heavy lifting work.

Movember Fundraising

I am an active participant in Movember's annual fundraising event, since I was 16. It's about raising money, spreading awareness, and donating to others.

Social Activity

Abertay Poketay Secretary

During my 2nd year of university, I became secretary of Abertay’s social Pokémon society “Poketay”. With a full shift in leadership, we were able to take a dying society of 6 active members. Shifting the focus from internal group activities to networking with other societies. Bringing in over 20 new active members at the end of the first year and winning the most improved society award.


Abertay SAL Drama

I am currently a passionate member in Abertay’s drama society, acting in community created stage shows and helping construct props. I also volunteered to be the head costume/prop designer for SAL’s rendition of the Greek play Medea.


Boys Brigade

I was part of the Troon Boys Brigade sector; participating in the extra events, fundraisers, and volunteer work along with the weekly meetings.


Hobbies

Starting with a self-motivated exploration into game creation. I am invested in the creation of both 3D and 2D art, developing hobbyist level skills in both Blender and Autodesk Fusion 360. Along with this, my recent ventures into 3D printing have allowed me to improve my skills with physical prototyping.

Horology

A collector’s hobby, my research into the world of watch making has introduced me to unique and fascinating works of mechanical engineering. Driving me to tinker and modify some of the pieces I collect. Seen in the photo on the right, modifications were made to the watch: giving the originally sand blasted casing a subtle polish; replacing the glass with sapphire; and adjusting the balance wheel to be 1.6s more accurate.

Weight Lifting

It is important to stay healthy, and weightlifting is my preferred method to keep active. However, as this is a heavily researched field challenging my knowledge and preconceived notion on this subject with new information is how I stay motivated in this activity.
